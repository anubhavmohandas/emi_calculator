The {{ emi | number:'1.2-2' }} syntax utilizes Angular's built-in number pipe for formatting.
The format string '1.2-2' specifies the desired output:
1: Minimum number of integer digits (always shows at least one digit).
.2: Exactly two decimal places.
-2: No grouping separators (e.g., commas for thousands).
